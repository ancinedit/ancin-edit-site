Welcome

Wedding planning that feels fun, intentional, and surprisingly calm.

At Ancin Edit, we believe wedding planning should feel fun, intentional, and surprisingly calm. Our approach blends creativity with organization, thoughtful details with big-picture vision, and structure with flexibility so the process feels just as beautiful as the day itself.

Founded by Justine, who transitioned from nearly a decade in New York City’s fashion industry to wedding planning, Ancin Edit brings a unique blend of editorial eye, strategic organization, and calm, intentional execution to every celebration.

Ancin Edit collaborates closely with a community of talented florists, photographers, musicians, and designers, weaving fashion, design, and storytelling into each wedding. No two celebrations are the same, and our goal is always to create something that feels effortless, personal, and true to each couple’s vision. Whether offering full planning, partial planning, or day-of coordination, we take the stress off your shoulders, handling the details so you can stay present, relaxed, and fully enjoy the day.

If you’re dreaming of a wedding that feels elevated but easy, intentional yet fun, and completely your own - welcome. We’re so glad you’re here!
