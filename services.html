The Process

Discovery

Once your date is secured, we begin by getting to know you — your style, priorities, and how you envision your celebration feeling. We’ll talk through what’s most important to you, what you want to avoid, and how you’d like your day to unfold. From there, we outline your planning vision and establish a thoughtful approach to your investment, focusing on the elements that matter most.

Plan & Design

With a clear vision in place, we begin building your celebration from the ground up. This includes venue scouting, vendor sourcing, budget guidance, and full design direction. From mood boards to layout planning, every detail is thoughtfully curated to create a cohesive and elevated experience. We manage logistics, communication, and decision-making so planning feels seamless and enjoyable.

Final Stretch

As your wedding approaches, we refine every detail. We finalize timelines, confirm vendors, review logistics, and ensure all moving pieces align. We step in fully as the main point of contact, allowing you to shift your focus from planning to celebrating.

Execution

On your wedding day, we oversee every element from setup through breakdown. We manage vendors, guide the timeline, troubleshoot behind the scenes, and ensure your vision comes to life seamlessly — so you can be fully present and enjoy every moment.
